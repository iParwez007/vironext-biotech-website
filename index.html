About Us

ViroNext Biotech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is an emerging biotech startup focused on bridging the gap between academic virology and real-world industry applications. Founded by a trained virologist, ViroNext delivers expert-led research consulting, advanced training programs, and diagnostic support to empower students, laboratories, and biotech organizations.

Our Services

Virology Research Consulting

Project design, experimental planning, viral assays, data analysis, and manuscript support for academic and industry research.

Education & Skill Development

Hands-on training, online courses, workshops, and certification programs in virology and molecular biology.

Diagnostic & Lab Support

SOP development, quality control, molecular diagnostics consulting, and lab setup guidance.

Scientific Writing & Grants

Manuscripts, thesis support, grant writing, and regulatory documentation for biotech and academia.
